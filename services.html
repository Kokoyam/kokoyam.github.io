Our Services

Full Dental Checkup and Diagnosis

We provide a thorough checkup of your periodontal health and look for cavities, tooth decay, and loose or misaligned teeth. After your diagnosis, we'll recommend a custom plan based on your personal situation and insurance plan.

Teeth Whitening

For teeth whitening, we use a piezo ultrasonic teeth whitening system that offers a quiet, painless, and comfortable experience. This process will eliminate stains, tarter, and other discoloration as well as prevent cavities and gingivitis by using Peridex oral rinse.

Zoom Whitening

By using a Zoom UV whitening system, we're able to revitalize and whiten your teeth in around 45 minutes.

Dental Implants

By using the latest laser oral surgery technology, we're able to reduce the pain and recovery time traditionally associated with dental implants. This process is quick and easy, and the implants will feel as natural as your own teeth.

Ceramic Fillings

Compared to other fillings, ceramic fillings are one of the most natural looking, durable, and safest because of the non-toxic and non-allergenic traits.

Cavity Prevention

Consult us and make a cavity prevention plan that works for you.

Crowns

Excessive wear from crowns can result in overly sensitive teeth, a cracked tooth, damaged gums, misalignment, and pain. By using the latest materials and techniques, we can avoid excessive wear and keep your teeth and gums healthy.

Porcelain Veneers

Improve the look of your teeth with porcelain veneers. These veneers noticeable improve your teeth's color, alignment, and shape.
